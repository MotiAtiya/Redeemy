# Privacy Policy — Redeemy

**Last updated:** April 30, 2026

---

## Who We Are

Redeemy is a personal finance management app that helps you track store credits, warranties, subscriptions, occasions, and documents. The app is developed and operated by Moti Atiya (contact: a.moti96@gmail.com).

---

## What Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** — used to identify your account
- **Display name** — optional, set by you
- **Profile photo** — only if you sign in with Google or Apple and your account has one

### Data You Enter
Everything you add in the app is stored securely:
- Store credits, gift cards, and vouchers
- Warranty records (product name, store, expiry date)
- Subscription details (service name, billing amounts, renewal dates)
- Occasions (birthdays, anniversaries, yahrzeits) including Hebrew calendar dates
- Personal documents (ID, passport, license, insurance — expiry dates only, not document numbers)
- Photos you upload for any of the above items

### Family Sharing
If you create or join a family group, your family's shared records and member names are visible to all members of that group.

### Device & Technical Data
We do **not** collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or location data.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide the app's features:
- Displaying your records across your devices
- Sending local notifications and reminders you configure
- Enabling family sharing with people you invite
- Allowing you to edit, delete, or export your own data

We do **not** sell, share, or use your data for advertising or marketing.

---

## Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in **Google Firebase** (Firestore and Storage), hosted in the United States. Firebase is subject to Google's privacy and security standards. Your data is protected by authentication rules — only you (and your family group members, if applicable) can access your records.

---

## Sign-In Providers

If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive only the basic profile information those services provide (email and display name). We do not receive access to your Google or Apple account beyond authentication.

---

## Data Retention & Deletion

- You can **delete all your data** at any time from Settings → Account → Delete All Data.
- You can **delete your account** at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.
- Family group data is removed automatically when the last member leaves.

---

## Notifications

Redeemy sends local notifications on your device based on reminders you configure (expiry dates, billing dates, occasions). These notifications are generated on-device and do not involve sending your data to any server.

---

## Children's Privacy

Redeemy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:
**a.moti96@gmail.com**
